Once upon a time, there was a HCMUT student who found himself stuck inside a dream with Deadline devil. In this dream, he becomes a knight who is never gonna give you up...
How to play?
W: Move up
S: Move down
A: Move left
D: Move right
Space: Attack (On beta)
Try to dodge the obstacles and collect as many coins as you can!
As you get more coins, the nightmare will become more and more intense! Watch out your step!
Before playing, please turn off Telex and use headphones for better experience.
